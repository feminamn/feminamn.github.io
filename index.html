About Me

I'm Femina - a performance marketer who genuinely loves the puzzle of paid social. What drives me isn't just hitting KPI targets (though I do love hitting them) - it's understanding why a creative works, what makes an audience convert, and how to build systems that scale without breaking.

I started my career as a business analyst, which gave me a strong foundation in data and client thinking before I moved fully into performance marketing. Over five years, I've worked across agencies and in-house-style environments, managing everything from ₹40Cr budgets at Dentsu to $11M+ monthly spends at Pixis - across markets including the USA, EMEA, KSA and Australia.

I hold Facebook Blueprint certifications in both Media Buying and Media Planning, alongside Google Ads certifications across Search, Display, Video, Shopping and Measurement. But beyond certifications, what I bring is a consistent track record of cutting acquisition costs, exceeding revenue targets and building the kind of rigorous testing culture that makes results repeatable - not lucky.

Currently based in Bengaluru. Open to high-impact roles where performance marketing is taken seriously.
